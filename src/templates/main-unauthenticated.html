How it works:

Survey Creators

  1. Create an account with us for free and login, if you have not already.
  2. On your user dashboard, click 'Create Survey'.
  3. When you are finished creating your survey, click 'Host Server' and provide the special survey code to your participants!
  4. Sit back, and watch those beautiful statistics and analytics pour in.
Host a survey »

Survey Participants

  1. Click 'Join a Survey'.
  2. You will be prompted for the survey code, which the host of the survey will provide.
  3. Once you have filled out the survey, you will be redirected to the live statistics and analytics page, which you can use to observe results from all other participants!
